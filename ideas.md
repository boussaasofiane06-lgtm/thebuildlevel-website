**Approach 3: Military-Grade Streetwear**
- **Design Movement**: Tactical minimalism — inspired by military spec sheets and underground streetwear drops
- **Core Principles**: Information hierarchy as design; functional beauty; every element earns its place; discipline reflected in grid precision

## Selected Approach: **Approach 2 — Dark Luxury Editorial**

This approach best captures the premium, cinematic quality of BUILD LEVEL while maintaining the aggressive energy of the brand. The editorial layout allows the product photography and motivational messaging to breathe, while the dark background creates the "luxury streetwear" feel referenced in the brief. Oswald's condensed weight delivers the bold, masculine typography required, while the crimson red accents provide the urgency and energy of the brand's call-to-action philosophy.
